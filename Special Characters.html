SPECIAL CHARACTERS

Special Saiyan, Wizard and Ninja Characters

Special Characters are unique characters which are based on their Anime counterparts. They are high powered and difficult to beat. To face a Special Character, one must indicate interest to an admin who will then prepare a battle with one. Beating a Special Character provides the victor with crowns & xps ranging from 80-120 and 10-13 respectively, although if you lose, you lose 40 crowns or 3xp(energy capsules and senzu beans are usable). A player can face a Special Character once every 3 days.
SAIYAN SPECIAL CHARACTERS

 VEGETA

The Saiyan Prince, born to the King and Queen of the now destroyed Planet Vegeta, he was bound to be a battle warrior from birth, with high power levels to back up his prince status. Vegeta escaped before the destruction of his planet by Frieza and trained long and hard to one day pay him back for destroying his home. He has not failed to be the fierce warrior he was born to be and has only grown from strength to strength with various power-ups along the way. He is definately one to fear even in his base form.

GOKU

Son Goku, Earth's strongest defender. Born to Bardock and Gine, he was sent from his home planet, Planet Vegeta, from a very early age because his parents feared for the his life as he had power levels which exceeded even that of the Saiyan Prince, Vegeta. He would grow to make the the legend of the Super Saiyan a reality as the first Super Saiyan for many centuries. He would not stop there, as he has grown stronger throughout the years and has been the first to unlock more and more modes, and most recently, he has been able to master the power of Ultra Instinct that even gods have been having trouble with.

BEERUS

The God of Destruction in universe 7, Beerus is a force to be reckoned with. He introduced Goku to the Super Saiyan God mode and was still able to outclass him without even using his full power. With the ability to destroy a whole planet with just a simple blast, Beerus is probably the strongest god of destruction in all the universes. Although he has been unable to master the Ultra Instinct which might give Goku a slight edge against him, Beerus is still though, a god of destruction with ruthless aggression and unbelievable power.
